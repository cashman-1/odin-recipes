scrambled eggs

delicious scrambled eggs are a quick and nutricious meal

ingredients

  • eggs
  • butter
  • salt
  • pepper
  • water
  • (optional) cheese,chives or salmon

how to make scrambled eggs

  1. heat on low heat butter in pan until melted
  2. crack eggs into a bowl and add a splash of water and your salt and pepper
  3. whisk until mixed
  4. pour into frying pan and stir continously until cooked through
  5. place on plate and add optional extras
